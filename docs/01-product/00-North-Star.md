## Mission

To construct a resilient, unified, and long-term digital environment that preserves the owner's knowledge, orchestrates personal projects, and acts as a central workspace, freeing the owner from reliance on commercial software.

## Vision

To build a Digital Headquarters that scales in capability and connectivity over a multi-decade lifecycle, evolving from a structured storage repository into an active cognitive engine and agentic workspace.

## Product Statement

Rifqi is a Personal Operating System designed for long-term scalability and maintainability. It functions as a structured single source of truth that powers a private workspace (the Studio) and a public presentation website (the Web), integrating knowledge modeling, project tracking, publishing pipelines, and AI-assisted workflows into a single monorepo.

## North Star Metric

The North Star Metric of Rifqi is the **Workspace-to-Web Semantic Linkage Ratio**. 

This metric evaluates the efficiency of knowledge transition from the private workspace to the public web interface. It is calculated by dividing the number of public-facing entities (Articles, Projects, Learnings) that contain active, bidirectional semantic references to internal, private reference models (such as Books, Goals, or Technology entities) by the total number of public-facing entities.

A high Workspace-to-Web Semantic Linkage Ratio guarantees that the public website is a direct, authentic reflection of active intellectual progress in the private workspace, preventing the public website from becoming a stagnant showcase.
